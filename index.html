Koyomi

A simple distributed job scheduler.

June 07, 2015

What is this?

Koyomi is a simple distributed job scheduler which achieves High Availability.

You can run koyomi worker on several servers. Then if one worker stops with a certain trouble, remaining alive workers will take after its jobs.

About page shows its supported platform and additional info about Koyomi.

How to use?

All you need are followings in a nutshell:

  • A couple of servers on which koyomi worker runs.
  • Datastore with high availability.

Currently supported datastore types are MySQL and SQLite.
